Common questions

A few things people ask before reaching out.

How quickly do we get started after I sign?
Onboarding takes about one week. First deliverables are usually in your inbox by the end of week two. I don't do slow ramp-ups — if you've signed, I'm already thinking about your brand.
Do I need to have my brand fully figured out first?
You need to have a clear offer and a sense of your audience. I'll help refine your positioning as we work together — but I'm not the right fit if you're still in the "figuring out what I sell" phase. That's a different kind of work.
What email platforms do you work with?
I'm most familiar with Flodesk, ConvertKit, and Klaviyo — but I can write for any platform. I deliver formatted copy that's ready to drop in. You or your team handles the actual send.
What if the writing doesn't sound like me?
Voice matching is built into the onboarding. Before I write anything, I spend time with your existing content — what you've loved, what felt off, how you actually talk about your brand. After month one, most clients tell me they stopped revising much.
Can I upgrade to a higher tier if I need more?
Yes. If your needs grow — more volume, more platforms, a launch sequence — we can discuss a scope adjustment. I keep things flexible once we have a working relationship.
